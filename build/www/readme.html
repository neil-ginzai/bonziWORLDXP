What the hell is this?

BonziWORLD XP (Experience Plus) is a node.js and socket.io based chat client evolving jojudge's BonziWORLD with new features, quality of life improvements, built in apps, and more. BonziWORLD XP features everyone's most hated loved purple gorilla. The client also uses speak.js (no memory leaks) to provide text-to-speech voices.

What are the chat commands?

  • /speed [speed] - Change your voice's speed.
